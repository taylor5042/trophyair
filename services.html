Our HVAC Services

Professional heating, cooling, and fireplace solutions

With 30 years of HVAC experience, Dave Taylor and the Trophy Air team provide expert heating, cooling, and fireplace services. From new installations to emergency repairs, we're here to keep your home comfortable year-round.

Heating Services

Keep your home warm and comfortable all winter long with our comprehensive heating services.

  • Furnace Installation: New high-efficiency furnace installations to reduce energy costs
  • Furnace Repair: Fast, reliable repairs to get your heat back on
  • Furnace Replacement: Upgrade your old system for better efficiency
  • Maintenance: Regular tune-ups to prevent breakdowns and extend system life
  • Heat Pumps: Energy-efficient heating and cooling solutions

Cooling Services

Stay cool during the hot summer months with professional air conditioning services.

  • AC Installation: New air conditioning system installations
  • AC Repair: Quick diagnosis and repair of cooling issues
  • AC Replacement: Upgrade to a more efficient cooling system
  • Maintenance: Seasonal tune-ups for optimal performance
  • Emergency Service: When your AC goes down, we're here to help

Fireplace Services

Add warmth and ambiance to your home with professional fireplace installation and service.

  • Gas Fireplace Installation: New gas fireplace installations
  • Fireplace Repair: Get your fireplace working properly again
  • Fireplace Maintenance: Annual inspections and cleaning
  • Fireplace Inserts: Upgrade your existing fireplace
