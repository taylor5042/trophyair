About Trophy Air

30 years of HVAC excellence

Trophy Air is owned and operated by Dave Taylor, bringing three decades of hands-on HVAC experience to every job. We're committed to providing professional, honest, and reliable heating and cooling services.

Meet Dave Taylor

With 30 years of experience in the HVAC industry, Dave Taylor founded Trophy Air to provide quality heating and cooling services with a personal touch.

Dave is known for his professionalism, expertise, and friendly demeanor. As one customer put it: "Dave is professional and caring! Has loads of experience and some funny jokes too."

When you call Trophy Air, you're getting a seasoned professional who takes pride in every job—whether it's a simple repair or a complete system installation.

Our Values

  • Quality Workmanship: Clean, professional installations and repairs done right the first time
  • Honest Service: Upfront quotes with no hidden fees or surprise charges
  • Responsive: Quick response times, including same-day service when you need it most
  • Customer First: We treat every home like our own and every customer like family

What We Do

Trophy Air provides comprehensive HVAC services including:

  • Heating: Furnace installation, repair, replacement, and maintenance
  • Cooling: Air conditioning installation, repair, and service
  • Fireplaces: Gas fireplace installation and service
  • Emergency Service: When something doesn't smell right, turn it off and give us a call!

We work in all spaces—big or small—and take pride in delivering clean, professional results every time.
